How It Works

Set it up once.
It works forever after that.

No software to install. No training needed. Your team just saves the flight number at booking time — ArrivIQ does everything else from that moment onwards.

01

Customer gives flight number

When a customer books, they give their flight number. Your team links it to the car registration. That's it — nothing else to do.

›
02

ArrivIQ watches the flight

From that moment, ArrivIQ tracks the flight every 5 minutes using 3 live data sources. Your team don't need to check anything manually.

›
03

System decides when to alert

ArrivIQ works out the perfect moment to send the alert — 45 minutes before landing. If the flight is delayed or diverted, it alerts the driver straight away.

›
04

Driver gets a WhatsApp

The driver receives a WhatsApp message with the flight status, terminal, landing time, passenger name and car reg — everything they need in one message.

Every UK airport covered

Heathrow, Gatwick, Manchester, Edinburgh and all 40+ UK airports. Your drivers get alerts no matter which airport the customer flies into — from day one, no setup needed.

If a flight is delayed, diverted or cancelled, your driver gets an instant WhatsApp — not a notification 3 hours later. We check 3 live data sources every 5 minutes so nothing slips through.

Works for drop-offs too

Not just return pickups. When a customer drops their car before flying out, ArrivIQ tracks their outbound flight and alerts your team at the right time — no more staff standing around waiting.

Driver gets everything in one message

Flight status, terminal, landing time, passenger name, car registration — all in a single WhatsApp. No back-and-forth calls. No confusion. Just one clear message and the driver knows exactly what to do.

Full record of every alert sent

Every WhatsApp logged with exact timestamp, flight status and confirmation it was delivered. Useful if a customer ever disputes a missed pickup — you have proof of exactly what happened.

Fits around how you already work

No need to change your booking system. ArrivIQ works alongside whatever software you use today. Larger operators can connect via our full API or use white-label branding under their own name.
